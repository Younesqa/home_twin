==================================================
SOLAR PAGE IDEA
==================================================

This is a simple demo feature.

The user can sell solar energy to the municipality and receive instant wallet balance.

Do not make it realistic or complicated.
Do not use real sensors.
Do not use external APIs.

Assumptions:
- The system generates 0.05 kWh per minute.
- The municipality buys 1 kWh for 0.60 NIS.
- The available solar energy increases over time while the user has solar panels.
- For demo simplicity, calculate available energy based on last_solar_update timestamp.

Example:
If 10 minutes passed:
10 * 0.05 = 0.5 kWh available

If 60 minutes passed:
60 * 0.05 = 3 kWh available

Price:
available kWh * 0.60 NIS

The user can choose how many kWh to sell.
They cannot sell more than available energy.

After selling:
- subtract sold kWh from available solar balance
- add money to wallet balance immediately
- add wallet transaction:
  type = solar_sale
  amount = money earned
  description = "بيع طاقة شمسية للبلدية"
